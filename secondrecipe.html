Hot Coffee recipe

let's learn to make a simple hot milk coffee ☕


Ingredients

  1. Water(100ml)
  2. Milk(60ml)
  3. 1½ tsp of Coffee powder
  4. ½tsp of Sugar

Instructions

  1. Add coffee powder into a Cup
  2. Add warm water to to Cup
  3. Boil the milk
  4. Add milk to the cup
  5. Add Sugar
  6. Mix with a spoon for 1 Minute

Now Enjoy a Hot cup of Coffee ☕!

Ingredients

For the dough

  1. 1 Cup all-purpose Flour
  2. Â½ tsp Sugar
  3. Â½ tsp Salt
  4. Â½ tsp Baking powder
  5. 2 tsp Oil
  6. Water (as needed)

for the toppings

  1. 3-4 tbs Pizza sauce
  2. Cheese(Mozzarella)
  3. Onion(Thin slices)
  4. Tomato(Thin slices)
  5. Capsicum (Thin slices)
  6. Chilli flakes and Oregano

Instructions


    Make the dough
  1. In a bowl mix flour,salt,sugar and baking powder
  2. Add oil
  3. Slowly add water
  4. Cover and rest for 10-15 minutes
    5. Shape the dough
  5. Roll the dough into a round base
  6. Take a pan (tawa)
  7. Place it on a cold pan
    8. Cook the base
  8. Cook on low flame
  9. When bubbles appear,flip it
  10. Cook the other side lightly
    11. Add toppings
  11. Add holes with a fork
  12. Spread pizza sauce
  13. Add cheese
  14. Add vegetables
    15. Cook
  15. Cover the pan with a lid
  16. Cook on very low flame for 10 minutes
  17. When cheese melts the pizza is ready
    18. Serve
  18. Add Oregano and Chilli flakes
